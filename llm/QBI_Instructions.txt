-------------------------------------------------------
CONTEXT: 
Calculate the Federal Qualified Business Income (QBI) deduction for the 2025 Tax Year. 
Incorporate updates from the One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA).

INPUT DATA REQUIRED:
1. Filing Status (Single, MFJ, MFS, HoH)
2. Total Taxable Income (TI) - Before QBI deduction and net capital gains.
3. Qualified Business Income (QBI) - Net pass-through income.
4. W-2 Wages paid by the business.
5. UBIA (Unadjusted Basis Immediately after Acquisition) of qualified property.
6. Business Type: SSTB (Specified Service Trade or Business) or Non-SSTB.
7. Net Capital Gains (including qualified dividends).

LOGIC & THRESHOLDS (2025):
- MFJ Thresholds: $394,600 (Lower) | $494,600 (Upper) | $100,000 (Phase-in range)
- Single/Other Thresholds: $197,300 (Lower) | $247,300 (Upper) | $50,000 (Phase-in range)

CALCULATION STEPS:

STEP 1: INITIAL CALCULATION
- Tentative Deduction = 20% * QBI.

STEP 2: TEST INCOME AGAINST THRESHOLDS
- IF TI <= Lower Threshold: 
    Final Deduction = Tentative Deduction (No wage/SSTB limits apply).
- IF TI > Upper Threshold:
    A. If SSTB: Deduction = $0.
    B. If Non-SSTB: Deduction = Greater of:
       i. 50% * W-2 Wages
       ii. (25% * W-2 Wages) + (2.5% * UBIA)
