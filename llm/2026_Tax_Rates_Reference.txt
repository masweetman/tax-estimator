2. STANDARD DEDUCTIONS (2026)
- Married Filing Jointly: $32,200
- Single / Married Filing Separately: $16,100
- Head of Household: $24,150
- OBBBA Senior Deduction (Age 65+): $12,000 (MFJ) / $6,000 (Single) 
  *Phase-out starts at $150k (MFJ) / $75k (Single) MAGI.

3. DEDUCTIONS & MILEAGE (NEW FOR 2026)
- SALT Cap (State and Local Taxes): $40,400 (Increased from $40k)
  *Phase-down begins at $505,000 MAGI (MFJ); floor is $10,000.
- IRS Business Mileage Rate: 72.5 cents per mile
- IRS Medical/Moving Mileage: 20.5 cents per mile
- Charitable Deduction (Non-Itemizers): $2,000 (MFJ) / $1,000 (Single)
  *Subject to a 0.5% AGI floor for all filers.

4. FAMILY & EDUCATION CREDITS
- Child Tax Credit (CTC): $2,200 per child (Partially refundable to $1,700)
- Dependent Care Credit: Up to $6,000 for 2+ children (Max 35% of expenses)
- 529 Contribution (Gift Tax Exclusion): $19,000 (Individual) / $38,000 (MFJ)
- 529 Superfunding (5-Year): $95,000 (Individual) / $190,000 (MFJ)

5. RETIREMENT & SAVINGS LIMITS (2026)
- 401(k), 403(b), 457 Limit: $24,500
- IRA Contribution Limit: $7,500 (Catch-up 50+: +$1,100)
- HSA Limit (Family): $8,750 (Self-only: $4,400)
- HSA Catch-up (Age 55+): $1,000

6. SURCHARGES (THE "250K MAXIMUMS")
- Net Investment Income Tax (NIIT): 3.8% on income over $250,000 (MFJ)
- Additional Medicare Tax: 0.9% on wages over $250,000 (MFJ)

7. CALIFORNIA STATE TAXES (2026 ESTIMATED)
- CA Standard Deduction (MFJ): $11,720
- CA SDI (Disability Insurance): 1.3% (Increased from 1.2%, No wage cap)
- CA Dependent Credit: $488 per dependent
- CA Personal Exemption: $157 (Single) / $314 (MFJ)
- CA Young Child Tax Credit: $1,117 (Qualifying children under 6)
- CA 529 Deduction (SB 529): $10,000 (MFJ) / $5,000 (Single) 
  *NEW: Only for Scholarshare accounts; income limits apply ($150k MFJ).
